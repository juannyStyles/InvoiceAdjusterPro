Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 5.28.2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how our app handles data when integrated with QuickBooks.

Data We Access

Our app only accesses the fields that you explicitly select within QuickBooks invoices. No other data is accessed or stored.

Data Storage

We do not store any personal, financial, or business data from your QuickBooks account. All data processing is performed in real-time and locally during your session.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties. We maintain a strict no-sharing policy.

Security

We use secure authentication provided by Intuit and comply with their guidelines to protect your data during interaction.

Changes

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users on this page with the revised date.
